Multiple Inductive and Conductive Sweep Procedure

Inductive Sweeps described below shall be perfomed using at leasttwo different line locating instruments(Multi-frequency and single freequency) to reduce risk of an instrument malfunction failing to locate an unknow undergrouity.

  • Inductive Sweep#1: On existing KM pipelines, confirm location and mark centreline of the pipeline. One new construction. perform an inductive sweep to clear the proposed trench line.
  • Inductive Sweep#2A: Perform an inductive sweep around the perimeter of the excavation area (or section of the excavation area).
  • Inductive Sweep#2B: After completing inductive sweep #2A performs an inductive sweep crossing the excavation area every five (5)feet until the entire excavation area has been swept.
  • Inductive Sweep#3: Perform an inductive sweep crossing the excavation area in a 'Zig Zag' pattern every ten (10) feet until the entire excavation area has been swept indicate the area has been swept by writing on centerline stakes inductive sweep date name
  • If only a section of the excavation area has been swept then repeat. Steps#1.#2& #3 on the next section until 100% of the excavation area has been swept.

Note#1: If there is any doubt that the excavation area was not property swept repeat the inductive sweep procedure until there is no doubt.

Note#2: If there is any doubt that the excavation area was not property swept repeat the inductive sweep procedure until there is no doubt.

Note#3: If there is any doubt that the excavation area was not property swept repeat the inductive sweep procedure until there is no doubt.
